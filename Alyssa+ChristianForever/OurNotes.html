An Eternity Ahead

From Dancing in the Moonlight
To playing with our might
To Flying to Venus or Mars
It's been quite the sight.

But between you and the stars
I'd pick you,
Who shines more bright

Like 2 shooting stars watched closely
Our Celestial Orbits have crashed
Unlike any other
Because me and you are meant to be

Tulip Tn The Weeds

I found these tulips in the weeds
Quickly my mind thought of thee
In the great sea of tares
There is no beauty greater or fair

Though this tulip dies to a seed
Soon we'll plant one for the eternities
Through rain, or drought, or hot beating rays
If I'm with you, come what may

So if the universe tears you from me
And I below the ocean sea
I'll swim the ocean sea
To spend eternity with my Lyssie.
